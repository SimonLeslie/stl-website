About

Hi. I'm Simon Leslie. This is my website.

A bit of background about me. I was born in London and have lived in the UK for most of my life but between the age of 10 and 18, I lived in France. I went to high school there, at a school where French was the main language. After passing the French baccalaureat, I returned to the UK for university. I studied history at the University of Cambridge and then law at Nottingham Law School.

For over a decade, I worked at an international law firm called Jones Day. After finishing my training contract, I qualified as a solicitor. I worked in the Global Disputes department and was involved in a number of important and high profile cases.

At the end of June 2014, I left Jones Day. Since then, I've been enjoying some time off. I've been travelling, reading, catching up with friends and family, and learning to program again. It has been great.

Now, I'm looking for a job again.
